About Me

I have been a nerd for as long as I can remember, and I love to code!

I began writing code for the first time as a kid, messing around with html, and making cool contraptions in Garry's Mod Wire Mod. After studying anthropology at California State University, Fullerton, I found that I wanted to go back and pursue my interest in code. I took the time to learn some python and computer science concepts on my own, and then decided to attend the Full-Stack Flex Web Development Bootcamp through the University of California, Irvine. Now, I have the skills to create awesome looking websites and apps, databases, APIs and logic!
